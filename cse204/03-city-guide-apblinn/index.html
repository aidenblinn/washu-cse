Places to go

Klyde Warren Park

Klyde Warren Park is a 5.2-acre public park in the Arts District of Downtown Dallas. First opened to the public in 2012, the park is known for the delicious food trucks that flank the park on all sides. Personally, I think they're great!

So if you're hungry and looking for a good place to walk around, look no further! You can grab some tacos or a slice of pizza at one of the many food, bring a frisbee with you and toss it with a friend, or just take a seat at one of the many tables.

The park is open daily from 6am to 11pm.

Source: Google Maps

Things to do in Klyde Warren Park:
  • Grab a bite at one of the food trucks
  • Take a stroll on a nice spring day
  • Use a Lime or Bird scooter to scoot around the park


Best places to get a coffee

  1. Ascension Coffee: This place has great espresso and an amazing menu of breakfast and lunch foods.
  2. Houndstooth Coffee: Houndstooth Coffee has a gorgeously designed interior and a great americano.
  3. Fiction Coffee: Fiction Coffee has a cozy vibe and a room in the back to do homework.
